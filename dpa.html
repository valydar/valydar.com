Data Processing Agreement

Last updated: July 2026

1. Purpose

This Data Processing Agreement ("DPA") forms part of the agreement between Valydar Software B.V. ("Processor") and the customer ("Controller") for the use of Valydar's identity verification services. It governs the processing of personal data under GDPR and applicable data protection laws.

2. Scope of Processing

Processor processes personal data on behalf of Controller to provide identity verification services, including:

  • Document images (passports, ID cards, driver's licenses)
  • Selfie images for biometric face matching
  • Extracted identity data (name, date of birth, document numbers)
  • Verification results and risk scores

3. Obligations of the Processor

  • Process personal data only on documented instructions from Controller
  • Ensure that persons authorized to process personal data are bound by confidentiality
  • Implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures
  • Assist Controller in responding to data subject rights requests
  • Delete or return all personal data upon termination of services
  • Make available all information necessary to demonstrate compliance

4. Sub-processors

Processor may engage sub-processors for infrastructure and hosting. Processor shall ensure sub-processors are bound by data protection obligations no less restrictive than this DPA. A list of sub-processors is available upon request.

5. International Transfers

Personal data may be transferred outside the European Economic Area. Processor ensures appropriate safeguards are in place, including Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) where required.

6. Security Measures

Processor implements the following technical and organizational measures:

  • Encryption of data in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256)
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Incident response procedures
  • Employee training on data protection

7. Data Breach Notification

Processor shall notify Controller without undue delay (and within 48 hours) after becoming aware of a personal data breach affecting Controller's data.

8. Contact

For DPA inquiries: privacy@valydar.com
