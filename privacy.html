Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. Introduction

Valydar Software B.V. ("Valydar", "we", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our identity verification platform and related services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information necessary to provide identity verification services:

  • Account information: Name, email address, company name when you register for API access
  • Verification data: Document images, selfie images, and extracted identity data processed on your behalf
  • Usage data: API call logs, timestamps, and verification results for billing and analytics
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, and device information for security and fraud prevention

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our identity verification services
  • To process verification requests submitted by our customers
  • To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents
  • To communicate with you about your account and our services
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. Data Processing

When you submit verification data through our API, we act as a data processor on your behalf. You retain ownership of all verification data. We process this data solely to fulfill your verification requests and do not use it for other purposes without your consent.

5. Data Retention

Verification data is retained for the period necessary to fulfill the requested verification, after which it is securely deleted unless otherwise required by law or your service agreement.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest, access controls, and regular security audits.

7. Your Rights

Under GDPR and applicable data protection laws, you have the right to access, correct, delete, and port your personal data. Contact us at privacy@valydar.com to exercise these rights.

8. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries: privacy@valydar.com
